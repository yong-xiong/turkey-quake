Daily Covid Vaccination in China

Data from China's National Health Commission (NHC) and Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), strating from March 2021 when the NHC began to release daily vaccination numbers. The data is automatically updated everyday.

The data shows a great surge of vaccination in early December 2022 after the country scraped "zero-Covid" policy.
